Teaching

Wildlife Population Science

In Spring 2018 I taught the lab section for Wildlife Population Science at Auburn Univeristy. This course combined basics of population ecology and dynamics with estimation of population parameters, with an emphasis on management and conservation applications. I had fun creating Shiny apps to help students explore population models and create stochastic projections for population viability analyses. Below are links to the apps and some other material.
